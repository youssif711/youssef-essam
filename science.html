Mobile computing is any type of computing that uses internet or intranet connections and connections from them, such as WAN, LAN, WLAN..etc. There are at least three different categories of mobile computing elements:

Laptops
Laptops, lightweight and affected modules including a full character set keyboard, notebook computers, notebooks, etc.
Cell phones
Cell phones have the ability to send and receive e-mails and correspondence and are not limited to voice communications, mobile phones, smart phones, etc.
Wearable computers
Wearable computers, mostly limited to functional keys and primarily founding software agents, watches, bracelets and necklaces.
